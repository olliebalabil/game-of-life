Game of Life

Try these presets!


This website provides an audible representation of Conway's Game of Life. Create and play your own patterns by clicking on the grid, or explore randomly generated patterns. A sound is produced when a cell becomes live (i.e. turns black). In essense, the further down or further right a cell is, the higher it is. As you travel from left to right across a row, you travel through the notes of a pentatonic scale, while as you descend down a column, the frequency of each cell ascends by a fourth.
